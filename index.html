About AVAN & Associates

Trusted financial advisors since 1998

Founded in 1998, AVAN & Associates has grown from a small accounting practice to one of the most respected chartered accountancy firms in the region. Our team of highly qualified professionals brings together decades of experience across various industries and sectors.

We pride ourselves on our commitment to ethical practices, innovative solutions, and personalized service. Our approach combines traditional accounting excellence with modern financial strategies to help our clients navigate complex financial landscapes.

As a client-focused firm, we take the time to understand your unique business needs and goals. Whether you're a startup looking for financial guidance or an established corporation needing complex audit services, we have the expertise to support your success.
